## The Decade That Reorganized Everything - Without Replacing It

The 2000s didn't start with a bang. They began with adjustments.

**Genres didn't disappear. They shifted their weight.**

In this 68-minute conversation, Daniel and Annabelle explore a decade that reorganized the past instead of erasing it.

## What You'll Discover

**You'll learn:**

- How pop became architecture through Max Martin's precision
- Why R&B turned vulnerability into tone, not drama
- How hip hop became the culture's operating system
- Why global sounds became normal, not exotic

- **Early 2000s Uncertainty** - Madonna, Destiny's Child, Radiohead navigating a changing industry

## The Real Story

The 2000s didn't have one dominant sound.

They made hybridity normal. They gave artists more control. They changed how music lives in daily life.

**They taught us to listen inward.**

This isn't a timeline. It's a journey through the small musical choices that changed everything.
